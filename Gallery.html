Our Pride – Success Stories

Celebrating the achievements of our talented dancers from across India

Priya Rajan

Kuchipudi Maestro

Priya has performed Kuchipudi across 15 countries and received the prestigious Sangeet Natak Akademi Award. Her innovative choreography blends traditional Kuchipudi with contemporary themes.

"Natyam Academy gave me the foundation to express our cultural heritage through dance on global stages."

Leela Menon

Mohiniyattam Exponent

Leela has revived the ancient art of Mohiniyattam and established her own dance school in Kochi. She has trained over 200 students and performed at UNESCO cultural events.

"The academy's dedication to preserving Kerala's dance traditions shaped my artistic journey."
