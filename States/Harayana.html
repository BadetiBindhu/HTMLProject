Traditional Dance Forms of Haryana

Discover the rhythm and spirit of Haryana's rich folk traditions

Ghoomar

Originally from Rajasthan, Ghoomar is also popular in Haryana. It features graceful twirling movements by women in colorful traditional attire.

Phag

Phag is a vibrant folk dance performed during the Holi season. Men and women celebrate the harvest with energetic movements and joyful songs.

Loor

Loor is performed by girls during the spring festival of Holi. It includes clapping, foot tapping, and singing, reflecting love, nature, and joy.

Jhumar

Jhumar is a slow-paced dance from western Haryana, often performed by men during weddings. It reflects happiness and community bonding.

Dhamal

Dhamal is a traditional dance by men of the Ahir community. Performed during festivals, it celebrates victory and valor with robust energy and drumbeats.
