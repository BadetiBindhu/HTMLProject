Traditional Dance Forms of Andhra Pradesh

Celebrating the vibrant culture and rhythm of the state

Kuchipudi is a classical Indian dance form from Andhra Pradesh known for its graceful movements, strong narrative expression, and dramatic character portrayal.

Burrakatha

Burrakatha is a traditional storytelling dance form that combines narration, drama, and music, usually performed by a trio. It conveys social and historical themes.

Dappu Dance

Dappu is a folk dance accompanied by a hand-held drum, performed during village festivals and celebrations. It’s known for its rhythm and energetic movements.
