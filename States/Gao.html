Traditional Dance Forms of Goa

Experience the vibrant culture and festive spirit of Goa through its folk dances

Fugdi

Fugdi is a Goan folk dance performed by women during festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi. It features circular formations, rhythmic clapping, and vibrant energy.

Dekhni

Dekhni is a semi-classical Goan dance performed by women in traditional attire. It combines Indian and Western musical elements and portrays feminine grace and local themes.

Mando

Mando is a graceful musical dance that blends Indian and Western traditions. It is usually performed in formal gatherings and accompanied by poetic Konkani songs.

Corridinho

Corridinho is a Portuguese-inspired folk dance performed in Goa. It involves couples dancing in sync to lively tunes, often in traditional European costumes.

Dhalo

Dhalo is a ritualistic dance performed by women, especially in rural Goa. It is usually held over 10 days and includes folk songs, prayers, and social bonding.

Ghode Modni

Ghode Modni is a martial dance that simulates horse-mounted warriors. Dancers wear traditional attire and perform with swords and shields to celebrate bravery and valor.
